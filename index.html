About Us

we believe that mental health is a fundamental aspect of overall well-being. Founded with a vision to create a society where mental health is prioritized and supported, we are dedicated to providing compassionate care, education, and resources to empower individuals in managing stress and achieving mental wellness.

Mental Health Support

Provide counseling services and support groups for individuals struggling with mental health issues.

Stress Management

Offer workshops and resources for stress reduction and coping strategies.

Mental Health Awareness

Educate communities about mental health issues and provide resources for support.

Awareness Program

The Awareness Program actively educates various communities, based on their needs regarding mental well being and coping with emotional distress.

Distress Helpline Program

Distress helpline program provides a confidential, non advisory space for those feeling low, and/or suicidal via its telephonic service, walk-in facility and distress mail.

Suicide Survivor Support Program

The Survivors support program provides survivors of suicide with a non-judgmental environment, and builds self sufficiency within communities to respond to distress and suicide.
